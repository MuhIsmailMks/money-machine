About

Magic Money Machine (MIM) is a decentralized stablecoin pegged to the US Dollar. Built on the Abracadabra protocol, it allows users to mint MIM by using interest-bearing crypto assets as collateral. MIM brings stability, flexibility, and true ownership in the world of DeFi.

Tokenomics

📊 Supply

- Circulating Supply: Dynamic, depends on minted and burned tokens
- Max Supply: Unlimited (backed by collateralized assets)
- Stability: Maintained through over-collateralization and liquidation mechanisms

💠 Distribution

- No pre-mine or team allocation.
- All MIM enters circulation only when users deposit supported assets as collateral.
- Fair and transparent minting process through the Abracadabra protocol.

⚡ Utility

- Stable medium of exchange across DeFi platforms.
- Trading pair on multiple DEX and CEX.
- Borrowing & lending opportunities.

How To Buy

STEP 1: Get Ethereum (ETH)

Buy ETH from a centralized exchange (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, etc.) or have it in your crypto wallet (like MetaMask).

STEP 2: Connect Your Wallet

Open a DeFi platform that supports $MIM (e.g., Uniswap, Curve, or Abracadabra). Connect your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.).

STEP 3: Swap ETH for MIM

- Choose ETH as the token you want to trade.
- Select MIM as the token you want to receive.
- Enter the amount and confirm the transaction.

STEP 4: Confirm & Store Safely

Once the swap is complete, $MIM will appear in your wallet. Make sure you add the official contract address to track your balance correctly.
